CCCM Cluster Somalia

Detailed Site Assessment (DSA)

These site profiles display the data collected by CCCM Cluster partners in Somalia. This tool aims at providing CCCM partners and other humanitarian actors essential information on services provision at site level to improve humanitarian assistance in IDP sites (this is currently a beta version of the site profile website). More information

The DSA is the outcome of the CCCM Information Management Technical Working Group comprised of the CCCM Cluster, REACH, IOM Displacement Tracking and Monitoring Unit, UNHCRâ€™s Protection Returns Monitoring Network, NRC, SomRep, Protection Cluster and Shelter Cluster. The DSA collects site level information with the overall objective to strengthen the predictability and effectiveness of multi sectorial interventions at site level and/or areas of concentration of sites. Information is collected through key informants at sites, figures are to be taken as indicative. As of January 2018, it covered 1,751 sites in 22 districts. If you would like to get further information or provide us with feedback, kindly email cluster coordination (k.ziga@iom.int).
